**Project Vision:**
To create a bilingual web application where a user inputs a personal or professional goal (e.g., "Learn to code," "Get fit," "Start a business"). The app then uses multiple AI models (Gemini, Claude) to generate a structured, motivational, and highly detailed weekly action plan. 1.  **Bilingual Brain:** Perfect support for both English and Arabic (RTL layout).
2.  **Model Switching:** The user can choose which AI model generates their plan and see the difference.
